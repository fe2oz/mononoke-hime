Prologue

A long time ago, this country was surrounded by dense forests, where ancient gods lived.

At the eastern end of the country, there was a village where a tribe called Emish lived in hiding. It has been about 500 years since the Emish tribe lost the fight against the Yamato court and disappeared to the back of history. The story begins in this village.
